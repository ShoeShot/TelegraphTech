About

Hi, I’m Jordan—founder of Telegraph Tech. Raised on the North Island, I grew up tinkering with computers, learning through trial, error, and a lot of curiosity. That early curiosity grew into a career in tech support, where I’ve spent years helping people solve problems and get the most out of their devices.

Now based in the Comox Valley, I’ve built Telegraph Tech around the values that matter most to me: honesty, reliability, and genuinely helpful service. I’ve worked with countless happy clients—whether it’s fixing a sluggish computer, setting up a new TV, or giving advice on an upgrade, I’m here to make your tech work for you.

Tech doesn’t have to be a headache—reach out anytime, and I’ll help you get it sorted.
